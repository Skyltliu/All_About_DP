similar logic as best time to buy and sell stock 1, except we can buy & sell multiple times.

Example:
Input: prices = [1,2,3,4,5]
Output: 4
Instead of thinking it as buy on day 1 and sell on day 5, think of it as
buy on day 1, sell on day 2, buy on day 2, sell on day 3, so on and so forth.
You will notice the result also comes out as 4.

In the DP solution, however,
we can reuse the code from best time to buy and sell stock 1, but in the 
holding case of the recurrence relations, instead of -prices[i], we roll over the profit from the previous days, 
and subtract prices[i]
