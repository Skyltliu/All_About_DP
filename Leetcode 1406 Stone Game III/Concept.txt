Use 1d dp, backward for loop
dp[i] = best score difference at index i

Base case:
dp[n] = 0, at index n, there are no stones left, so 0.
if i+k covers all, then what Bob takes won't matter, therefore it is 0.

Recurrence Relations:
current total sum of Alice - the current total sum of Bob.
